About Me!


Hello! My name is Rae and welcome to my portfolio! I hold a B.A. in English with a Business Minor from Southeastern University. I served as Secretary and Treasurer for Sigma Tau Delta, Line Editor of the Sciences for The Oracle, and graduated Cum Laude.

I also volunteered at a local Lakeland church, Victory Assembly of God, as a second and third grade Wednesday night teacher for 2 years. When I'm not working, I like to go camping, hiking, and off-roading with my friends!
